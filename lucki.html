Iphones (2007-17)

  • Iphone 8

    • he iPhone 8, released by Apple in September 2017, marked a significant evolution in the company's smartphone lineup. Boasting a sleek glass design with a 4.7-inch Retina HD display, the iPhone 8 introduced a durable aerospace-grade aluminum frame, available in three colors: Silver, Space Gray, and Gold. Under the hood, the device featured the powerful A11 Bionic chip with a neural engine, enhancing performance and supporting advanced machine learning capabilities. One standout feature of the iPhone 8 was its improved 12-megapixel camera, equipped with optical image stabilization and a larger sensor for better low-light performance. The device also supported 4K video recording at 60 frames per second, providing users with a versatile tool for capturing high-quality photos and videos. Additionally, the front-facing 7-megapixel camera received upgrades to deliver sharper selfies and support the new Portrait mode for professional-looking photos. Wireless charging made its debut with the iPhone 8, leveraging the device's glass back. This feature, along with the device's water and dust resistance, added to the overall convenience and durability of the smartphone. The iPhone 8 continued to support Touch ID for secure biometric authentication. In summary, the iPhone 8 combined a stylish design, powerful performance, advanced camera capabilities, and new features like wireless charging, making it a noteworthy addition to Apple's lineup and a popular choice for users seeking a reliable and feature-rich smartphone experience.
